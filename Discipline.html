The unspoken law of a passionate forex trader:

A perspective from the other side of the retail world:

Who?

My name is Daniel, i'm the founder of Money Session brand and my day to day business is Forex Trading. Everything started when i was 19 years old when my passion for numbers took me on a jurney in the financial markets. For me, it all began with a question i had when i started my journey " Why are the percentages of unprofitable traders so unbelievably high?"

It just didn't make sense to me that over 99% of traders will experience a net loss in the market. Out of more than 15 000 accounts opened at my broker, there would always only ever be 200-300 active accounts at once, with the rest of the accounts being inactive - or to put it another way, they incurred trading losses / margin called. So, the survival rate was around 1-2 %. The common phrase that "95% of retail traders are losers" isn't wrong. In fact, if anything, the situation is probably worse, and i can certainly verify the statistics!

I was extremely pazzled by this fact. I had heard about this statistic before, from just about everywhere in the trading world. However, it was only when i was actually working at the brokerage that i first became interested in understanding why. I thought, from a common statistical sense: "Yes, retails are always at a disadvantage, because they have to pay for spreads/ slippage; but even with these elements factored in, the stats should be somewhere around 40% profitable / 60% unprofitable, and not to the extreme of 1 out of 100. Also, Smart Money, like banks and hedge funds, have to pay the spreads, but they are not on the losing side of the market, so there must be something more going on here."And so, i set out on a journey to uncover the answer to this puzzle wich i putted together with a easy strategy to follow in the Money Session Program

Why?

My goal was to win this game because i don't want to end up in a 9 to 5 job with an average life. My mum was hustling nearly all day long when i was a kid and most of the time when she came home, she was tired from all that work stress.

So i decided at a very early age, that i want to live life on my own terms. This was my main motivation. Of course, i had some materialistic dreams as well, but living life on my own terms was really the most important one. In school, i had a lot of struggles because of my problem with authority. The only optin i had, was beeing my own boss. But how would i do that at such a young age, without any special degree for running a business + limited investment capital ? After going through a artist career i did my research accordingly and then i found Forex Trading. I felt in love with the idea, to let my money work for me, so i wanted to master this skill. Several years later i can say that i have mastered this skill. I make a living off trading and live my life on my own terms. I can do what i want every day, that's freedom.

It was not always like that. Especially in the first years, there were a lot of failures. I blew up a couple of accounts while i was working in silly jobs. I have put nearly all my earnings from the side-hustles into my trading account because i wanted it so bad. Now i know that this was one of my biggest problems back then. I wanted it so bad. Every time i took a loss i wanted to have it back as fast as i can, so i was not really trading. I was just gambling most of the time, and whenever i lost a trade, i was feeling the pain. It was a rollercoaster ride of emotions. Even if it was a lot of pain sometimes, i really enjoyed the thrill of Trading. The idea of letting the money work for me inspired me.
